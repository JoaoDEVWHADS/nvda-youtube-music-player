# YouTube Music Player for NVDA
### 1. Searching
1. Open the add-on (`NVDA+Alt+Y`). You will land automatically in the edit field.
2. Type the song or artist name.
3. If you want to change the provider, press `Tab` to select "YouTube Music" or "YouTube".
4. Press `Enter` to search.

**Navigation Tip:**
- Press `Escape` in the results list to return to **provider selection**. If you select a provider (the same one or a different one) and press `Enter`, the search will be repeated automatically.
- Press `Escape` **again** (at provider selection) to return to the **edit field**.
- **Quick Shortcut:** If you are in the results list and want to instantly return to edit your search, just press `Escape` **twice**.
### 2. Navigating Results
1. Results appear in a list. Use `Up` and `Down` arrows.
2. To play, press `Enter` on the desired result.
3. To see more results, press `Tab` to the "Next Page" button (or `Shift+Tab` for "Previous Page") and press `Enter`.

**Tip:** In the results list, you can press `Escape` to go back and choose your preferred provider again. If you press `Escape` once more, you will return to the edit field to perform a new search.

### 3. Controlling the Player
When the song starts, the player window opens automatically.
- To close the player and return to search, press `Escape`.
- If you want to search for another song **without stopping the current one**:
  1. Tab to the "Results" button and press `Enter`.
  2. Press `Escape` twice.
  3. You will return to the search field. Type the new song and repeat the process.

## Player Commands

### Volume
- Up Arrow: Increase volume
- Down Arrow: Decrease volume

### Track Navigation
- Right Arrow: Forward 1 second
- Left Arrow: Back 1 second
- Shift+Right Arrow: Forward 10 seconds
- Shift+Left Arrow: Back 10 seconds
- Ctrl+Right Arrow: Forward 60 seconds
- Ctrl+Left Arrow: Back 60 seconds

### Speed and Pitch
- Page Up: Increase speed and pitch
- Page Down: Decrease speed and pitch
- Ctrl+Page Up: Increase pitch only
- Ctrl+Page Down: Decrease pitch only
- Ctrl+Up Arrow: Increase speed only
- Ctrl+Down Arrow: Decrease speed only

### Playback
- Space: Play/Pause
- Escape: Close player (returns to search)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Usage Tip:** When skipping tracks, **do not press "Next" repeatedly too quickly**. The player needs time to load the new song.
>
> **Recommendation:** Wait for NVDA to announce the title of the song **starting to load** before clicking "Next" again. This is an MPV limitation and there may be a delay of about 2 seconds depending on your internet.

## âš™ï¸ Configuration

You can configure the add-on by going to **NVDA Menu > Preferences > Settings > YouTube Music Player**.

### Authentication (Cookies)
To access age-restricted content or your personal premium features, you can provide a `cookies.txt` file in Netscape format.
1. Log in to YouTube Music in your browser.
2. Use an extension like "Get cookies.txt LOCALLY" to export your cookies.
3. Save the file and select it in the add-on settings panel "Cookies File" field.

**Troubleshooting:** If the cookies stop working (e.g., after logging out) or you encounter errors, simply **clear the path** from the generic settings field to disable authentication.

## ğŸ“š Advanced Usage

### Search Results Navigation
- Results are displayed in pages (usually 20 items per page).
- Use the **Previous Page** and **Next Page** buttons at the bottom of the dialog to browse more results.
- **Shortcuts:**
  - `Escape` on list: Returns to provider selection.
  - `Escape` again: Returns to search edit box.
  - `Enter` on list: Plays the selected track.

### Quick Player Controls
When the focus is on the **Player** button within the Results dialog, you can control playback without opening the full window:
- `Space`: Play/Pause
- `Left` / `Right`: Seek -10s / +10s
- `Up` / `Down`: Volume +/- 5%
